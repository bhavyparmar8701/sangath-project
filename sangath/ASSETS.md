# Image Assets Required

Place the following images in the `public` folder:

## Hero & Banner Images
- `hero-banner.jpg` - Main hero section background image (recommended: 1920x600px)

## Product Images
- `product-sugar.jpg` - Sugar product image
- `product-spices.jpg` - Spices product image
- `product-grains.jpg` - Food grains product image
- `product-tea.jpg` - Tea product image
- `product-pulses.jpg` - Pulses product image
- `product-agro-feed.jpg` - Agro feed product image

## Other Images
- `export-world-map.jpg` - World map showing export destinations
- `quality-certification.jpg` - Quality certification image
- `team-photo.jpg` - Management team photo

## Logo
- `logo.png` or `logo.svg` - Company logo (update Header.jsx to use actual logo)

## Notes
- All images should be optimized for web (compressed, appropriate format)
- Recommended formats: JPG for photos, PNG for logos, SVG for icons
- Ensure images are high quality but optimized for fast loading
